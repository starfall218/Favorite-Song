FADED

Written by Alan Walker & Iselin Solheim

“Faded” by Alan Walker, it’s an emotional voyage that resonates deeply with listeners worldwide. Through its haunting melody and poignant lyrics, “Faded” explores themes of loss, longing, and the search for identity. The recurring plea, “Where are you now?” echoes the universal human experience of searching for something—or someone—that seems just out of reach. The song’s ethereal quality, amplified by Iselin Solheim’s haunting vocals, creates a dreamlike atmosphere, drawing listeners into a reflective state. Whether it’s a lost love, an abandoned dream, or a fading memory, “Faded” speaks to the heart of our shared human experience, making it a timeless anthem for anyone who has felt the sting of what once was but is no more.

About

Alan Olav Walker was born 24 August 1997. He was originally known by his stage name DJ Walkzz. Walker is a Norwegian record producer and DJ. The recognizable logo consists of intertwined letters "A" and "W", the initials of his name, was designed by Walker himself in 2013. He also uses a hoodie and face mask resembling the concept of black bloc on stage to create the rest of the image. In the first promo pictures his management handed out, Walker's face was barely visible at all.
